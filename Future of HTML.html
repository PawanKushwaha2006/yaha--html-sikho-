❓ Will Watchman Jobs End?
Short Answer: No, but the role will change.

Jobs that will remain:
1. Tech-Savvy Guards - Who can operate CCTV, AI systems, drones. Salary will triple.
2. Armed Response Team - AI will alert, humans take action. Police-type training.
3. VIP/Personal Security - More trust in humans. AI will support but humans decide.
4. Rural Areas - Traditional watchman will continue for 20+ years in villages.

Jobs that will decrease:
1. Gate duty only - AI will replace
2. Register writing - Everything digital/biometric
3. Unskilled guards - Those without training will be replaced
⚠️ Important Advice for Current Watchmen
If you're a watchman now, don't worry, but upskill yourself:
1. Learn basic computer/CCTV operation - Free on YouTube
2. English/Hindi typing - Registers are going digital
3. Smartphone use - Everything shifting to apps
4. PSARA license + Security training - Government making it mandatory
5. First Aid, Fire Safety certificate - Increases value

Those who adapt will earn ₹50K+. Those who don't will be replaced.
